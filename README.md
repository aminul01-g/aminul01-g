I'm a Computer Science & Engineering student from Bangladesh 🇧🇩 focused on **applied AI/ML**. I build LLM-powered applications, RAG systems, and computer-vision pipelines — and I document everything I learn in public. My goal is to become a research-capable AI engineer who can also ship and scale models in production.

## 🔭 Currently

- 🤖 Building **agentic LLM apps** with LangGraph, Groq, and vector databases
- 👁️ Working on **object detection & tracking** with YOLOv8 and ByteTrack
- 📚 Deepening my fundamentals in **transformers, fine-tuning, and MLOps**
- 🌱 Logging the whole journey — Python basics to agentic AI — in [my-learning-hub](https://github.com/aminul01-g/my-learning-hub)
